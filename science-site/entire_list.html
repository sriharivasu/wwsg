The Tree of Science

From the curvature of spacetime to the grammar of a sentence, every line below is a branch of the same trunk: organized human inquiry. This index gathers the recognized domains, disciplines, and fields of science into one classification tree — numbered, described, and arranged from the broadest domain down to its most specific subfield.

Click any branch to open it, or use Expand all to see the whole tree at once. Search filters by name or description. Boundaries between fields are drawn for clarity — in practice many disciplines overlap, borrow methods from one another, and resist tidy classification.
